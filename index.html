ABOUT US

Born to Race. Built to Win.

Team Maximus was forged in the relentless pursuit of speed. Founded by a group of obsessive engineers and visionary drivers, we didn't just enter Formula 1 — we redrew the boundaries of what a racing team could be.

Every bolt, every aerodynamic curve, every strategy call is driven by one singular obsession: to be first across that line. Our shadows don't just follow — they become the story.
